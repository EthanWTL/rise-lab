About the lab. The RISE Lab focuses on trustworthy representation learning (robustness, fairness, causality, transferability) for computer vision and natural language understanding. We build models that generalize, explain, and serve people, drawing on user modeling and interdisciplinary collaborations. Our methods power applications in educational measurement, bioinformatics and biomedical informatics, and public health.

  • Core areas: Trustworthy Representation Learning, Computer Vision, Interdisciplinary Applications
  • Affiliations: University of Viriginia School of Data Science
